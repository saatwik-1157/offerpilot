Questions

Everything you're wondering

Do you guarantee me a job?

No — and be wary of anyone who does. We guarantee volume and quality: 25–35 tailored applications a day to roles you're matched for. The interviews and offers are yours to win. 82% of members see screening calls within 2–3 months.

Is this legal on my F-1/OPT?

Yes. You are the applicant on every application — we act on your behalf to prepare and submit them, the same way a career coach or assistant would. We're a job-application service, not an immigration advisor; consult your DSO for status-specific questions.

How is this different from a staffing agency?

Agencies place you and take 20–30% of your salary, often for years. We charge a flat $100/month, take zero commission, and you keep 100% of your offer. You also stay in control of which offers you accept.

Who actually writes the resumes?

Our RezForge™ engine drafts a role-specific resume for each application — tuned to the keywords and skills that role screens for — and your specialist reviews it. It was built with input from 60+ HR professionals.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime from your dashboard. We refund you for the unused days left in your billing period. If we determine your profile isn't a fit before we start, you get a full refund.

Will employers know I used a service?

No. Applications go out under your name, from your profile, exactly as if you submitted them yourself. Recruiters see a strong, tailored application — nothing else.
