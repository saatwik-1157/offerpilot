Refund Policy

We want the math to work in your favor. Our refund policy is simple:

  • Cancel anytime. When you cancel, we refund you on a pro-rated basis for the unused days remaining in your current billing period.
  • Fit guarantee. If, after reviewing your profile, we determine we cannot effectively serve your job search, we will issue a full refund and let you know before applications begin.
  • No salary commission. On every plan, we take zero percent of your salary. Your offer is entirely yours.
To request a refund or cancel, email us and we'll process it within 5 business days.

Privacy Policy

Your job search is personal. Here's how we handle your data:

  • What we collect. The profile information you provide (name, contact details, resume, target roles, skills, and visa status) so we can apply on your behalf.
  • How we use it. Solely to prepare and submit applications, tailor resumes, and communicate with you about your search. We never sell your data.
  • Who sees it. Your dedicated specialist and the systems required to run your applications. We do not share your data with third parties except as needed to submit an application you've approved.
  • Your control. You can update your profile anytime and request deletion of your account and data.
This demo stores all data locally in your browser — nothing is transmitted to a server.

Terms of Service

  • What we do. OfferPilot is a job-application service. We prepare and submit applications on your behalf; you remain the applicant on every submission.
  • What we don't do. We are not a staffing agency, recruiter, or immigration advisor. We do not guarantee interviews, offers, or employment, and we cannot give visa or legal advice — consult your DSO or an attorney for status-specific questions.
  • Your responsibilities. You confirm the information you provide is accurate and that you're authorized to work in the roles you target. You approve the scope of your search during onboarding.
  • Company names & logos. Employer names shown on our site illustrate where members have interviewed and do not imply any affiliation or endorsement.
